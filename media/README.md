# Media — where your photos & videos go

Drop files into these folders, then tell Claude. Photos get **optimized**
(compressed → WebP, lazy-loaded) and wired into the site automatically.

## Folders
| Folder | What goes here | Naming |
|---|---|---|
| `events/` | One photo/flyer per event | **match the event id**: `mirage.jpg`, `meltdown.jpg`, `reunion.jpg`, `phoenix-rising.jpg`, `dawn-patrol.jpg`, `poolhouse-2.jpg` |
| `highlights/` | Recap gallery (crowd shots, drops, sunrise) | anything — 6–9 looks best |
| `bar/` | The 44 themed-night photos | anything |
| `brand/` | Logos, hero background, misc brand art | anything |

## Photos — what works best
- Straight off your phone is fine — they'll be compressed for you.
- Landscape ~1600px wide for events/hero; square ~1200px for the gallery.
- Send as many as you want; we'll pick the strongest.

## 🎬 Videos — do NOT put video files in here
Self-hosted MP4s are heavy, eat bandwidth, and play inconsistently. Instead:
1. Upload to **YouTube** or **Vimeo** (free, plays everywhere, never slows the site).
2. Paste the link into **`videos.md`** (or just send it to Claude).

That keeps the site fast forever — the "do it right once" rule for video.
